                <p>Key Survey Insights:</p>
                <ul>
                    <li>**Primary Goal:** The vast majority of users visit the site with the primary goal of adopting a cat.</li>
                    <li>**Discovery:** A significant number of users discover "It's Meow or Never" through social media and Petfinder.</li>
                    <li>**Pain Points:** A recurring theme in the responses was the difficulty in finding information, with the website's design often described as "outdated" and "cluttered."</li>
                </ul>

                <p>The combined findings were synthesized using an affinity map to identify core user needs and formulate clear problem statements.</p>
                <p>User Personas:</p>
                <p>Based on the research, three primary personas were identified: "The Hopeful Adopter," "The Compassionate Donor," and "The Dedicated Volunteer."</p>
                <p>Problem Statements:</p>
                <ol>
                    <li>**Hopeful Adopters** need a clear, centralized way to view available cats and understand the adoption steps because the current process is fragmented and causes confusion.</li>
                    <li>**Donors** need easily accessible donation options with clear statements on impact because the current links are hard to find and lack motivating context.</li>
                    <li>**Community Members** need a prominent way to find information about upcoming events (like vaccine clinics) because this information is currently difficult to locate.</li>
                </ol>
            </div>
